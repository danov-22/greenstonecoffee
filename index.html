Our Story

Rooted in the Art of the Perfect Cup

Greenstones was born from a simple belief — that coffee is more than a morning habit. It's a ritual, a pause, a conversation starter. Since 2024, we've been sourcing single-origin beans from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Indonesia, roasting in small batches to bring out every note.

Our baristas aren't just staff — they're craftspeople who care deeply about temperature, grind size, and timing. Every shot pulled, every milk steamed, every cup placed on the counter is the result of that care.
